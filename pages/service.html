Academic Service

symBIOsis

In 2020, I helped co-found symBIOsis, a graduate student support and mentorship group in the Department of Biology.

The goal of symBIOsis is to improve the PhD student experience by providing resources for navigating graduate life. Among other things, we organize:

  • The BioBuddies program, which pairs incoming first years with upper-year mentors
  • Mock qualifying exams for second years
  • Advising meetings on topics like choosing thesis labs and doing taxes as a grad student
  • Social events (happy hours, game nights, movie screenings)

For two years, I was symBIOsis's "Remora": the committee member who kept everyone on track for events they'd signed up to run. Currently, I help organize events as a regular symBIOsis committee member.
